Infographics

I also created a few infographics exploring more tidbits about the shows that I learned throughout this process. Developing infographics is a learning process - it's how I retain new information and see relationships that I wouldn't have seen otherwise. The conclusions explained below are ones that stood alone previously, but when linked with data, they create the full story. I thought I knew a lot about the Muppets, but my own work proved just how little I knew and how much I want to keep learning and growing this project. More to come!
